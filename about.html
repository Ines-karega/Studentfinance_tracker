About Us

Student Finance Tracker

Version 1.0.0

Student Finance Tracker is a dedicated tool designed to help students manage their budget, track expenses, and plan for financial success. It provides a simple yet powerful interface to keep your finances in check without the complexity of full-blown accounting software.

Key Features

  • Track daily expenses and income.
  • Categorize transactions for better insights.
  • Visualize spending habits with dashboard summaries.
  • Mobile-friendly design for on-the-go tracking.
